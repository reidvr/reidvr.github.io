What is Emotional Intelligence?

One's ability to be comfortable with their own emotions and empathise with others.

How is it different to IQ?

IQ is more a measure of one's personal ability to perform a given skills set. EQ is a measure of how we manage emotions both internally and externally.

Why is it important?

Because we are social creatures. Having high EQ gives us the ability communicate effectively with others and with ourselves.
