Sprint 4 Core

confident are you with each of the Learning Competencies?

I have had experience with many of these in other languages and so I picked them up fairly easily.

Which parts of the this sprint made you feel cool?

Understanding how closures and IFFE's work. Basically javascripts way of making variables private.

Which parts of this sprint made you angry?

Trying to create good architecture. Its frustrating knowing their is a better a way but not knowing how to implement it.

How many times during this sprint did you want to give up? Why didn't you? Do you still think you might give up?

I never wanted to give up, but thinking about all the different technologies modern web devs use, it gets a little overwhelming. One step at a time I guess.

Do you still want to be a programmer? Why?

Ofcourse. It can be a real struggle sometimes trying to understand certain concepts and make thing work the way you want, but thats what makes the reward of climbing these obstacles so great.
